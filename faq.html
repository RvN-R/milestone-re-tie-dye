A: Each item is unique and a one off. We try our best to post items on our socials that are currently up for auction. So if you have seen something you like on our instagram between Monday 9am and Friday midnight that week, then you will be able to place a bid on it. However, if you are looking at something we posted in April and it's July then that item won't be available. We provide one off pieces, and as a result we will never reproduce, so if you have an oppertunity to bid, don't miss out.... bid !

A: Before we dye any of the garmets they are boiled washed with a small amount of vinegar. This increases the garmets ability to obsorb the dye. However, we are using natural dyes and as a result its been known on the first few washes for the dye to run. So as a precaution we suggest for the first four washes you wash the item individually just in case any dye runs. In additon we suggest you refrain from washing your Re Tie Dye item with whites.

A: Yes all garments are boiled washed, not only does this ensure hygiene but it increases the garements ability to accept natural dyes.

A: We aim to use cotton garmets for tie dye as they tend to take natural dyes better than other synthetic materials. However, we are commited to offering a range of unique garmets, not limited to material choice. Material informaiton will be included with other information per item.

A: We accept Visa Debit, Visa Credit and MasterCard. All credit and debit card holders are subject to validation and authorisation by both us and the card issuer, to maintain security and prevent fraud. We take security seriously, so your details will be safe with us.

A: Not to sound overly dramatic but you have 24 hours! Few obvious reasons for this, one is to avoid false bidding. It's unfair to bid on an item with a bid you have no intention on settling.
