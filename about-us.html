About Us

Re Tie Dye started at the beginning of 2021, the goal to provide one off unique pieces while having a minimal effect on our planet.

Why are we doing this?

The impact of the fashion industry on our envrioment can no longer be ignored. As an overall industry it's now seen as one of the top global poluters. As demand for throw away fashion has grown the damage to our planet has increased. Its estimated that 95% of the clothes we throw away could of been reused. So why can't we do things diffirently? We aim to supply the demand for fresh, new looks with existing unloved garmets?

Why natural dyes?

Dying clothing has always been an unpleasant process. As the fashion industry has grown, the demand for brighter colours, quicker dying processes and larger quantaties has produced the techiques our fashion industry uses today. We achieved the aesthetic results, but at what cost ? The chemicals used release fumes containing dangerous toxins. The process of disposing of these checmicals is expensive and challenging. As a result the by product of these chemicals if inproperly disposed off can have caterstopic results to the enviroment. So why used checmicals when there is an alternative? Humans have been dying clothing for thousands of years with natual dyes from a variety of different vegtables, fruits, plants and spices. We can achieve the same result as industrial dyes with less effect on the envroment, it just requires a bit more effort in the dying process!
