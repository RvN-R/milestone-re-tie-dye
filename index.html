How it Works!

  • We scour charity shops looking for garmets crying out for a new start
  • Using veg,fruits and plants we tie dye these garmets creating one off pieces
  • Every monday we put these garmets on our site shop and the silent auction begins
  • You then have 5 days to place bids on items that catch your eye
  • Midnight on Friday the auction ends and the winners will be emailed
  • Winners have 24 hours to pay for the item, otherwise they forfit there bid and the next highest bid gets awarded the garmet
  • That's it pretty simple, have a look at our FAQ's for any other questions!
