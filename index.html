So, how does this work?

  • Charity shops are scoured, to find pieces looking for a new start in life.
  • Using veg, fruits and plants we tie dye these garments creating one off pieces.
  • Items are launched on our site each Monday and the silent auction begins.
  • You then have 5 days to place bids on items whihc you have to have!
  • On Friday at midnight the auction ends and the lucky winners notified.
  • Winners have 24 hours to make payment, after which time the items will be offered to the next bidder in line.
